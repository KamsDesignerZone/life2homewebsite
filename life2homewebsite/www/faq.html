Frequently Asked Questions

What are the services offered by Life2Home?
At Life2Home we bring you complete interior designing turnkey solutions to you, we offer standard modular furniture designing services as well as consultancy and take care of the execution to give you your completed dream home.
What is Life2Home’s field of work?
To start with, we are putting forth our expertise in modular furniture and modular kitchens, we offer assistance during other scope of work involved, while also providing you management services for them, i.e, we provide you vendors from our preferred team for various scope of work such as false ceiling, electrical work etc., you are to pay the vendors directly for their labor and material resources while we do the supervision of execution until your project is completed and handed over to you.
Can I choose my own designs?
Yes, you will be shown and explained 10-12 standard pre-designed modules, you choose and finalize one which shall be taken up for execution.
Can I mix and match designs by using elements from different standard designs?
Yes, you have an option of doing so, we can also give you inputs/suggestions regarding those.
What will be the execution period after finalization of designs?
Approximately 6-8 weeks.
What cities does Life2Home operate in?
Life2Home delivers services PAN India, whilst being operational from Pune.
Can we get registered before visiting Life2Home office?
We recommend you to visit our office first and understand our concept, and work process before you finalize Life2Home for designing your house. The registration process starts after your approval and visit to Life2Home’s office.
Will I have a specific person assigned to my project?
Yes, you will be assigned a specific executive to your project who would be available for any assistance required during the project timeline.
What changes can I propose in the designs that I select for execution?
The only changes/adjustments made will be with regard to dimensions, colours and placement, other than that, customizations will not be offered. For customizations regarding interior design, we advise you to choose the KAM’s Designer Zone and it’s services.
What if we want customized designs according to our preferences?
For those seeking uniqueness and personalization, our bespoke services curated by LIFE2HOME are at your disposal. With us, you'll not only receive assistance during execution but also enjoy after-service support for a remarkable 2 years from the project's completion.
Can I surprise visit my site or the factory to inspect quality of work?
Yes, we believe in giving the client complete assurance and to maintain transparency we will co-operate in any quality checks you would like to do.
Does Life2Home undertake civil work, if required?
Yes, Life2Home also provides solutions for your civil work requirements. We provide you with vendors, you pat the vendors direct for their material resources and labour, while we directly supervise them to get your work done.
Do you design 1BHK apartment?
Yes! We design your houses, be it big or small, entire houses, or just a room, we do it all.
Can work be executed, if we are residing in the same property?
Yes, but the time period for completion cannot be committed because the contractors cannot start work for all rooms in parallel in such scenario. Please note that in such scenario, the false ceiling work and painting base coat makes the place absolutely dirty. If you have kids under age of 2 years or a pregnant woman in house, you are recommended not to get the interiors done while staying in the same flat.
How should we calculate end-to-end project timeline?
Project completion time depends upon adherence of timely payments.
What will the preferred mode of communication be?
For maintenance of record and efficient co-ordination, the preferred mode of communication is via the Whatsapp /Telegram group specifically made for your project.
Do you have a copyright on your designs?
Yes, all standard designs provided in any form are licensed under (KAM’s Designer Zone/Life2Home) with exclusive copyrights.
Does Vaastu really matter?
Vaastu Shastra may not be essential for living, but it is helpful for a better and healthier life. It is the science of environment you live in. The energy generating in the environment you live will define the energy you build in you and your mind. At Life2Home, we also have the right contacts to provide you vaastu consultation services.
What are laminates used for?
Laminates are widely used surfacing materials, suitable for both residential and commercial spaces. Laminate sheets can be used to enhance the appeal of all types of surfaces, including walls and furniture.
Is acrylic good for kitchen cabinets?
Acrylic is a non-toxic finish that gives a perfectly smooth texture and a high-gloss appearance to kitchen cabinets. This high-quality synthetic material comes with a perfect mirror-like finish that's shatter-resistant, and also does not chip or crack easily.
